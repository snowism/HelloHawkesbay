Located in the centre of picturesque Napier, this luxurious 5-star hotel is located in the center of the Art Deco Precinct. Originally built for the Hawke’s Bay County Council in 1909, The County Hotel is one of only two significant buildings to have survived the massive 1931 earthquake. The hotel presents a charming Edwardian Art Deco grandeur, preserving its elegant heritage.
